A Guide to Copping New Releases

As posted in reddit

For online, You could go straight to the supplier such as Nike, Adidas, Reebok, etc. Since they are official, they will be the most prepared for the release. Other retail sites that are not as big will carry them, but are more prone to going down. They can't handle that amount of traffic.

Didn't get lucky the first time? Keep checking sites for restocks. Restocks happen mainly when people ordered the wrong size or had no luck in the reselling market so they return the sneakers. It will never happen for the really big releases but I recently got lucky with a restock on the Nike More Uptempo Olympics. Don't give up!
